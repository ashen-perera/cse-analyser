## 🚀 How to Start
You do not need to install any complex software or set up a server. 
1. Navigate to your `d:\cse analizer ultimate` folder.
2. Double-click the `index.html` file.
3. The application will instantly launch in your default web browser (e.g., Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, or Safari).

## 📊 Core Features Guide

### 1. Live Trading Dashboard
* **Dynamic Index Tracking:** At the top of the dashboard, you will see the ASPI and S&P SL20 index values. 
* **Market Status Awareness:** The application automatically checks Sri Lanka Standard Time (UTC+5:30). If you open the app outside of standard CSE trading hours (9:30 AM – 2:30 PM, Mon-Fri), the market will pause, and a red **🔴 Market Closed** badge will appear.
* **Top Movers & AI Signals:** Watch stock prices flash and adapt in real-time. The AI Signal column will automatically flip between `STRONG BUY`, `SELL`, and `HOLD` depending on rapid price changes. Clicking any stock opens the **Analysis Modal**.

### 2. ATrad Profit Calculator
Tired of manually calculating broker fees? Head over to the **Portfolio** tab.
* Input your exact Buy Price, Quantity, and Target Sell Price.
* Click **Calculate Profit**.
* The system automatically calculates and subtracts the standard CSE/ATrad transaction fees (~1.12% per side) to give you your **True Net Profit**.

### 3. Space Group Company Suggestions
Click on the **💡 Our Suggestions** tab in the left-hand navigation menu to view expertly curated stock picks.
* Each entry contains a detailed **Analyst Take** explaining the methodology behind the trade.
* Clear **Entry**, **Target**, and **Stop Loss** prices are provided so you can set your ATrad orders accurately.

### 4. Interactive Watchlist
* You can add any stock directly to your personalized Watchlist from the Analysis Modal.
* Navigate to the **⭐ Watchlist** tab to monitor only the stocks you care about.
* Remove stocks from your list at any time with the "Remove" button.

### 5. Advanced Search & Charting
* **Search:** Use the search bar at the very top to locate specific CSE symbols (e.g., "EXPO", "JKH", "SAMP"). Clicking a search result immediately runs an analysis on that stock.
* **Charts:** The dashboard line charts map volatility smoothly. You can switch between `1D`, `1W`, `1M`, and `1Y` views to adjust the simulated timeframe.
